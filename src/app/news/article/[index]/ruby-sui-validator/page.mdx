We first met the Mysten Labs team at Sui Basecamp 2024 in Paris, where we were immediately impressed by their world-class expertise as ex-Meta engineers behind Move and Diem. Over the past two years, Ruby Nodes has been deeply involved in the Sui ecosystem - running testnet validators, supporting Walrus launch as storage and validation partner, operating genesis Seal servers and API, and providing Hypernet/SCION connectivity for secure node networking.

After all this groundwork, we're thrilled to announce that our Sui mainnet validator is now live. This milestone marks the culmination of our collaboration with Mysten Labs, securing the high-performance L1 that powers gaming, DeFi, and consumer apps at scale. From testnet contributions to full mainnet participation alongside Walrus, Seal, and IKA validation, Ruby Nodes is proud to be an end-to-end infrastructure partner in Sui's explosive growth.

The journey has been rewarding, and we're excited for what's next in this top-tier ecosystem.
